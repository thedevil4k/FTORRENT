Why FTORRENT?

Native Performance

Built with pure C++20 and FLTK. Zero heavy frameworks like Electron, ensuring maximum efficiency.

Elite Engine

Powered by libtorrent-rasterbar, the same engine used by qBittorrent and Deluge.

Multi-Threaded

Optimized architecture that takes advantage of all CPU cores without blocking the UI.

Ultra-Light

Extremely low RAM and CPU footprint. Dedicated to what matters: your downloads.

Real-time Limits

Apply bandwidth and resource limits instantly without needing to restart the application.

Expert Control

Granular control over DHT, PEX, LSD and advanced network settings for power users.
